Our Story & Values

Who We Are

The MABU AGROFARM Philosophy

Mabu Agrofarm is a forward-thinking, family-run agricultural enterprise dedicated to the ethical and sustainable farming of livestock. Specializing in high-quality cattle, sheep, and goats, we serve both the breeding and commercial markets.

Located primarily in Renostervlei, North West, with a secondary location in Vlakfontein, our farm is founded on a deep commitment to superior animal husbandry, biodiversity, and community service.

To consistently improve and maintain the genetic quality of our herds utilizing selective breeding programs focused on fertility and disease resistance.

Our Offerings

Quality Livestock & Services

MABU AGROFARM offers two distinct lines of service: supplying premium livestock for breeding programs and providing market-ready animals for diverse commercial and ceremonial needs.

Breeding Stock Supply

We supply high-quality breeding stock to other farmers looking to expand or improve their herds. Our focus is on genetics that ensure health, fertility, and strong commercial traits.

Cattle

Supply of young bulls and heifers from proven bloodlines, selected for rapid growth and superior maternal traits. We invest heavily in genetic diversity.

Sheep

Offering ewes and rams selected for their adaptability, high lambing rate, and excellent meat or wool quality.

Goats

Providing bucks and does that are hardy, prolific, and ideal for meat or milk production.

Commercial & Event Use

We understand the cultural significance and logistical needs involved in providing animals for special events. We offer a respectful and efficient service.

Cultural & Ceremonial Events

Supplying healthy, market-ready cows, sheep, and goats for weddings, funerals, traditional ceremonies, and community events with dignity and promptness.

Custom Selection & Delivery

Clients can select animals based on specific requirements. We offer flexible and dependable delivery services to ensure the livestock arrives on time.

About Us

Mabu Agrofarm is a forward-thinking, family-run agricultural enterprise dedicated to ethical and sustainable livestock farming. Specialising in high-quality cattle, sheep and goats, we serve both breeding and commercial markets.

Vision & Mission

To be the premier and most trusted livestock provider in the region, recognised for superior animal health, genetics and environmental stewardship.

Our Objectives

  • Excellence in breeding — improve and maintain genetic quality.
  • Sustainable growth — responsible farming & rotational grazing.
  • Reliable commercial service — supply for cultural and commercial events.

Our Services

We supply cattle, sheep and goats for breeding and commercial needs; we also offer selection and delivery for events.
